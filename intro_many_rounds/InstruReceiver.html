{{ block title }} The role of the Receiver {{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

Only the receiver chooses between the two bonus options. However, they do not know what the bonuses actually are. All they see is this:

Which option do you chose?

You can click on an option to see what it feels like.


They have one other piece of information, a message sent by the Sender.
There are two possible messages:

"Option A will earn you more money than Option B"
"Option B will earn you more money than Option A"

With only this information, the receiver choose between option A and option B. Regardless of what the message say, they can choose any option! Once they choice is made, all they will know is their own bonus. They will never know what the Sender earned or what was in the option not chosen.
