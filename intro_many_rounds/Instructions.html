In this experiment you will be matched with another participant from Prolific. Neither of you will ever know the identity of the other.

There are two roles in this study: The Sender and the Receiver.

Both of you can receive a bonus payment depending on your decisions. There are two possible set of bonuses. It looks as follows:

First the Sender learns what the possible bonuses are, Option A and Option B.

Then the choose between two possible messages to send to the receiver

The receiver reads the message.

The receiver chooses between Option A and Option B with the message as the only information they have.
