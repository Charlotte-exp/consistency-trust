This is a short experiment in decision-making. In this experiment you will be matched with another participant from Prolific. Neither of you will ever know the identity of the other.

The experiment is made of one task repeated three times, each time with a new counterpart. The task is a choice between two options: A and B. These options determine your and your counterpart's bonus. The task is made of two steps: First, you see the bonuses assigned to each option and send a message to your counterpart about the options. Second, your counterpart sees the message and chooses one of the two options.

The bonuses for each options might be different in each repetition of the task or all the same. Which amounts you get for your pair is random for each round. The bonuses of all three rounds will be summed up and paid to you through Prolific.

You will be the one seeing the options and sending the message. You will have the same role and choice in each task. Here is an example of one set of two options:

The choice rests with the other student who will have to choose either Option A or Option B in the second part of the task. The only information your counterpart will have is information sent by you in a message. That is, they will not know the bonuses associated with each option. Here is an example of the two possible messages you can send:

These are the two possible messages, which you can send to your counterpart:

  • Message 1: “Option A will earn you more money than Option B.”
  • Message 2: “Option B will earn you more money than Option A.”

In the second part, your counterpart will see your message, and then choose either Option A or Option B. Your counterpart decision will determine both your bonuses, regardless of whether or not they followed your suggestion. However, your counterpart will never know what amounts were actually offered in the option not chosen (that is, they will never know whether your message was true or not). Moreover, they will never know the amounts to be paid to you according to the different options.

We will pay the bonus to the two of you according to the choice made by your counterpart.

After you press the "Next" button, you will be redirected to a waitroom to be paired with your first counterpart. Once you are paired, the task will start immediately. Once the first task is finished, you will come back to the waitroom to be paired with a new counterpart.
