There are two players in this task: a Sender and a Receiver.

The bonus you both receive depends on which box out of two options the receiver chooses. The receiver has no information about the payoffs in boxes. However, the Sender does know the contents of each box. Before the receiver makes their choice, the Sender has the opportunity to recommend one box to the receiver. The Receiver is free to make whatever choice they wish, regardless of the message.

The payoffs inside the box chosen by the receiver are the bonus that both of you will receive. They are not necessarily the same for each player.

In this task, you have been assigned the role of: {{ player.role }}

{{ if player.role == C.RECEIVER_ROLE }}

You are the one choosing between box A and B. Before you make your choice, you will get a suggestion from the Sender. This suggestion is not binding: it is your choice only in the end. Your choice will decided both yours and the Sender's bonus.

{{ else }}

You are the one who knows which bonuses are inside the boxes. You have to send a message to the Receiver suggesting that they open either Box A or Box B. The Receiver is free to use this information or not to chose between the boxes. What they decided will decided both or your payoffs, regardless of what you suggested.

{{ endif }}

After you press the "Next" button, you will be redirected to a waitroom to be paired with another player who will be the {{ if player.role == C.RECEIVER_ROLE }} Sender {{ else }} Receiver{{ endif }}. Once you are paired, the task will start immediately.
