In this study, we’re exploring how people make cooperative decisions across a series of choices, with some conditions changing each time.

In each of {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds, you’ll choose between two options: “Cooperative” or “Selfish”.

The choices you make will affect your bonus as well as the bonus of a previous participant who participated in this study, as follows:

  • Selfish option: You receive {{ C.endowment }}, while the previous participant receives £0.
  • Cooperative option: You receive fewer than {{ C.endowment }}, and the previous participant receives more than £0.
The exact amounts awarded will vary each round.

Only one round will be chosen to count toward the final bonus. This round will be selected at random after you have completed all rounds. Each round has an equal chance of being selected. Only amounts from the chosen round will be added to your and the previous participant’s bonus payments.
