Thank you for taking part in this study!

In this study, you will interact anonymously with others via the computer. You will be faced with a series of decision-making situations. The entire study will take approximately {{ C.session_time }} minutes.

For your participation in the study, you will earn {{ session.participation_fee }} for simply participating. In addition, you can earn an additional bonus, the amount of which will depend on your choices and the choices of other participants.

In this study, there will be two different parts. In one part, we will be studying cooperative decision-making. In the other part, we will be studying financial decision-making. In each part you will make {{ C.half_rounds }} different choices.

One of the choices you make will be randomly selected at the end of the study to “count”; meaning, the choice you make in that round will influence the bonus you earn as well as potentially the bonus another participant earns.
