Thank you for taking part in this study!

In this study, you are are going to engage in an interaction with another participant. Each participant is assigned a role. They can either be the active player or the passive player.

In the interaction, there is the opportunity for each player to receive a bonus payment. This bonus will depend only on a decision taken by the active player. The passive player will not make any decision.

After this interaction, you might face any number of additional interactions. Each such interaction will work the same as the first one, except you will be paired with a new participant. Your role (active player or passive player) will remain the same. The bonuses from each interaction will be summed up and paid to you after you have completed the study.

You must stay on task at all times. Otherwise you will automatically be disconnected and lose your bonus.
