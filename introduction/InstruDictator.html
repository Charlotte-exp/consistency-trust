For this experiment, you have been randomly assigned the role of Decider.

This means that in every interaction you have a decision to make. In each interaction, both you and the receiver are allocated with {{half_pot}} each. You have the opportunity to take the money of the receiver or leave it. If you decide to take it, you will earn {{pot_money}} in that interaction and the receiver gets nothing. If you decide to leave it, you keep your allocated {{half_pot}} and so does the receiver. Receivers will only learn their overall earnings (across all the receiver's interactions), but not the individual decisions of each decider.

Remember, in each interaction you are paired up with someone different and make a new decision. What happened previously is in the past and you are allocated {{half_pot}} again.
